The Approach

More than coverage — a narrative

Anyone can point a camera at a stage. The difference is in the story.

Nationwide isn't just attending the National Urban League Conference — it's showing up for a community. My job is to make that visible: the handshakes, the keynote energy, the quiet leadership moments between sessions, and the pride of the Women's Empowerment Luncheon.

I shoot with cinema-grade cameras, dedicated audio, and intentional lighting, then craft each edit around a single emotional throughline. The result is content your executives are proud to share and your brand team can repurpose across LinkedIn, internal channels, and future recruitment.

Two days on the ground. Two films that keep working long after Nashville.

Scope of Coverage

Two days on the ground

Full mobile kit, run-and-gun ready, coordinated to your executives' schedule.

DAY 01
Thursday, July 30
12:00 PM – 3:00 PM
  • Executive arrivals, on-floor presence & brand-moment coverage
  • Candid leadership interactions and networking b-roll
  • Optional short-form soundbite / interview capture
  • Environmental & branded-space detail shots
DAY 02
Saturday, August 1
10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Women's Empowerment Luncheon — full-room energy & keynote moments
  • Executive remarks, reactions & audience engagement
  • Relationship & community-impact storytelling b-roll
  • Closing brand moments to bookend the films
Deliverables

Two finished films

Both fully edited, color-graded, scored with licensed music, and delivered ready to post.

FILM 01

Conference Brand Recap

A 90-second cinematic recap of Nationwide's presence across the conference — energy, leadership, and community in one shareable story.

FILM 02

Women's Empowerment Feature
